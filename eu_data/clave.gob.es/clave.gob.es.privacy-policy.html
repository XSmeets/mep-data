The web site of the Cl@ve systems uses the Google Analytics application, that installs cookies temporarily, with the only purpose of preparing statistical reports. For example, number of visits per site, visit hours and days, etc.

The Directorate for Information Technologies and Communication follows legal provisions and regulations on such regard set out by the Spanish Agency for Data Protection.

Specifically, the cookies which would be installed would be the following:

 
Cookie Purpose
__utma Used to differentiate users and sessions
__utmb y __utmc Used to determine new sessions/visits
__utmz Registers the origin of traffic

 

In order to browse the Cl@ve website it is not necessary to approve the installation of these cookies; you can disable them directly in your browser settings.
