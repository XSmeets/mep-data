      Page Content

      Effective: September 20, 2023. ​

      We collect your personal information as a user of the website and any other technological tools (hereinafter called "tools") operated by Ville de Laval. This Confidentiality Policy describes how we process your information. It also contains information about your rights with regard to your personal information and how you can exercise these rights. The processing of your personal information is governed by the Act respecting access to documents held by public bodies and the Protection of personal information.​​


      What personal information is collected and why?​

      When you contact us or use one of our online tools, we collect only the personal information that is necessary for processing your request.  Where applicable, we will indicate whether a request for personal information is mandatory or optional, and the consequences of your choice to provide or withhold any of this information. Some information is mandatory in order to respond to you (for example, on certain forms) or to provide you with a service. Measures that allow you to refuse such collection or to request that it cease, and the consequences that may result, will be indicated.

      ​​​Automatic collection of personal information

      ​Cookies

      During your visit, your browser exchanges data with our server. We use cookies to store certain information about your device in order to facilitate your navigation on our website as well as on our other digital platforms. A cookie is a small file stored on the device of the user of a website and its main purpose is to record information about the user and the operations carried out on the website in question. Cookies do not enable Ville de Laval to identify you.

      You may disable the use of these cookies at any time, using your browser options. However, this may prevent you from using certain online services or functionalities. Under no circumstances is this information used to identify individuals or their browsing habits outside Ville de Laval's website and other digital platforms.

      Usage statistics for marketing purposes

      We use behavioural tracking tools that enable us to follow the activity of visitors to our website and to other digital platforms. These tools make it possible for us to offer you targeted advertising based on your areas of interest and/or the content consulted. All this is done through the use of marketing cookies, provided that you have consented to the collection of your personal information through the interactivity between you and our website.

      We don't have access to data that would specifically allow us to identify you through targeted advertising. Cookies and advertising services are managed by third-party platforms.

      To whom is your personal information disclosed?​

      The personal information we collect on our website or through technological tools is confidential and only authorized staff members or a provider authorized by contract with Ville de Laval have access to it for the purposes of carrying out their responsibilities or their contract.

      We will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, unless authorized by law. 

      ​​​How long do we keep your personal information?

      We keep your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, subject to our retention schedule adopted under the Archives Act​.​​

      All individuals are encouraged to update their personal information to ensure that we have their accurate, complete and up-to-date information at the time of use.

      Who is in charge of ensuring the security of Ville de Laval's network?​

      A team in the Service de l'innovation et des technologies is in charge of the integrity and security of our website. No data is therefore communicated to an external service provider.
      For security and prevention purposes, data collected during your interactions with Ville de Laval is also sent to backup servers in Ontario.
