Overview

Please add your comments to this site -- we’re very interested in what you have to say. Government wants discussion sections to be a good experience for everyone, so here are a few simple guidelines to keep in mind.

  • be respectful -- no personal shots or defamatory statements.
  • avoid partisan comments. This is a place to discuss policies and initiatives of the provincial government, not a political party. There are lots of other places to voice your political preferences
  • express your own opinion
  • treat others as you would treat a guest in your home, with courtesy All discussions are moderated, which means that inflammatory or otherwise inappropriate posts will be removed.
