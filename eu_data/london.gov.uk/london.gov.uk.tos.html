Using Talk London

Talk London is City Halls online community. Anyone aged 16 can sign-up to get involved in our research to help shape City Halls policies and plans. The Talk London team will email you about new opportunities to have your say and keep you updated on how youre helping shape plans and policies. 

Please note that discussions taking place within the Talk London community will be monitored and analysed for City Halls research purposes. Find out more about how we process your personal data in our Talk London privacy policy.

Our community guidelines have been written to make everyone feel welcome to get involved in a constructive way. The guidelines may be changed at any time without notice, so please check them every now and then. If you continue to use the community well assume you agree with the new guidelines. 

If you have any questions about anything included in these guidelines, or would like us to explain anything in further detail, please contact us on talklondon@london.gov.uk and well do our best to answer your questions.
