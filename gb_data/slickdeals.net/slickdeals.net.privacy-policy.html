Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2023

Slickdeals, LLC (“Slickdeals,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) values its users’ privacy across the Slickdeals web site (Slickdeals.net), mobile site, and apps (collectively, the “Properties”). This Privacy Policy pertains to our Properties, the services that we offer on our Properties and your interactions with us outside our Properties other than through our browser extensions (collectively, the “Services”). We have implemented this Privacy Policy to provide you with information about how Slickdeals collects, uses, and discloses information about you. By visiting our Properties or using our Services in any other way, you are agreeing to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to our browser extensions, which have different practices. To learn about the information practices for our browser extensions, you should view the Extension Privacy Policy rather than this Privacy Policy. If you choose to use a Slickdeals browser extension in addition to other Services, then the information collected through the extension may be combined with information we collect through other Services, and we will use this combined information to provide both the extension and our other Services to you.

This Privacy Policy is primarily for users of our Services.  If you interact with Slickdeals in a business capacity (such when you partner with us to offer deals), please visit the “Business Visitors” section of the Privacy Policy below for information about how Slickdeals collects, uses, and discloses information about you.  Other sections of this Privacy Policy apply only to users.

What Types of Personal Information Does Slickdeals Collect from Me?

Slickdeals will collect the personal information you provide to us as a user, for example when you register, take a survey, sign up for notifications, participate in a sweepstakes, email us or otherwise send us your contact information. Personal information may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing and/or billing address, and other information that identifies you as an individual. You can use some of the Services without providing these types of personal information, but certain features may not be available. If you would like to update these types of personal information that you previously provided to us, you can log into your account or email us at info@slickdeals.net.

If you access our Services from a mobile or other device, we may collect a unique device identifier assigned to that device. We also collect information about your device’s precise location over time to provide you with more relevant deals, such as offering deals in real time for a store that you are visiting.

Each time you use our Services, Slickdeals also automatically collects other, technical information, which may be combined with other information we collect about you. This technical information includes, for example, your browser type, the URL of the previous web site you visited, your Internet Service Provider, your operating system, and your IP address. We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect such information as described below. We also use analytics and reporting technologies to collect records of your browsing and activities when using our Services, as well as other usage information such as your clickstream patterns, your preferences, and the dates and times that you use our Services.

If you receive email or texts from us, we will analyze related information such as whether you open the email or read the text, and whether you click on an ad or offer in the email or text.

To help us personalize the Services, we may offer you the ability to opt in to allow Slickdeals to automatically scan the emails received and sent in certain email accounts (e.g., Gmail). If you do, Slickdeals will scan your emails in order to collect and analyze information about your purchases over time, the marketing emails that you receive and how you respond to them, and your participation in loyalty programs. We will use all of this information in order to provide you with more relevant content and recommendations through our Services. For any information scanned from a Gmail account, Slickdeals complies with Google’s policies and restrictions on how we can use or share this information. Slickdeals can scan your emails only if you choose to enable this service by providing us with your email access credentials. You can use our Services without participating in email scanning.

When you provide us with your address, we may use third-party services such as the Google Places API to help complete your address information after you begin to type it. We will combine the address information that we collect through this service with any other information that we collect from or about you, and we will use, retain, and share it as described in this Privacy Policy. Additionally, this address completion service allows Google to collect certain information as described below.

We may collect information about you from third parties in other ways as well. If you log in via a third-party service like Facebook or Google, we will collect the information described in the third-party service’s request for authorization. This information may include your name, email address, gender, city, and state. You should review your privacy settings with these third-party services to be sure you are only sharing information you wish to share. Additionally, when you click on a link from our Services, we may receive information from merchants (and their affiliates) about your activities on their linked platforms, such as your purchases. We may use this information to support loyalty programs with merchants or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy. We may also obtain information, including personal information, about you from other third parties and combine it with the information that we collect from you.

What About Cookies and Web Beacons?

In addition, Slickdeals (and our third-party service providers) use “cookies,” a small data string our server writes to your computer, and other similar technologies to help us manage and analyze your interaction with our Services. These technologies can store information that we collect about you, and can collect information linked to your browser or device that we use for a number of purposes, including improving our Services, keeping count of return visits to our Properties or our advertisers’ or partners’ websites, collecting and reporting statistical information about our users, saving your password and login information, displaying advertising or content based on your interests (as further described below), and learning about how you interact with email or text messages and advertising content. We may also collect information about your search criteria and results, the date and time of your visit to our Properties, and your connection speed and IP address. Cookies may store information that identifies your device, like a hashed email address, but we do not use cookies to store individual identifiers like your name.

Third parties whose products or services are accessible or advertised via our Services may also use cookies on our Properties, and we encourage you to check their privacy policies for information about their information privacy practices.

You may be able to refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser; however, refusing to receive cookies will not stop all data collection and may prevent you from being able to use the full functionality of our Services.

Additionally, emails we send may contain a bit of code known as a “web beacon.” This code allows us to understand the time and date when you open an email and when you use a link within the email to visit a website. If you wish to disable our web beacons, you can do so by turning images “off” in your email software (e.g., Outlook, Outlook Express). Please see your email software instructions or “Help” for more information.

  • To customize and personalize the advertising and the content you see.
  • To personalize and improve your experience when using our Services.
  • To fulfill your requests for our Services.
  • To send you information about topics that we think will be of interest to you.
  • To alert you to the latest developments and features on our Services.
  • To enter you in our sweepstakes when you request that we do so and to award prizes.

  • For our research, recordkeeping, and reporting purposes.
  • To monitor our Services for compliance with our Terms of Service and policies.

 Interest-Based Advertising Choices

Slickdeals adheres to the Digital Advertising Alliance (“DAA”) Self-Regulatory Principles. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you use our Services, including viewing our emails or texts, and to serve ads on our behalf on non-affiliated properties. These companies may use cookies or other non-cookie technologies to collect information about your visits and activities on our Services, other online properties, and email and text campaigns over time and across different browsers and devices, in order to provide advertisements for us and others about goods and services of interest to you. This type of advertising is called interest-based advertising. The information collected by these third parties may include information about your browser or device including information that identifies your browser or device over time (including device identifiers and/or personal information which has been hashed or obfuscated).

You may visit https://www.aboutads.info/choices or http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp to learn more about interest-based advertising, to opt-out of this form of advertising on your web browser by companies participating in the DAA and Network Advertising Initiative self-regulatory programs, and to learn about the effect of an opt-out choice.

If you wish to opt-out of interest-based advertising in mobile apps on your device by companies that participate in the DAA’s AppChoices app, you may download that app onto your device and exercise your choice. AppChoices is available at the links below:

DAA AppChoices iOS

DAA AppChoices Android

You may also limit data collection and use by third parties for interest-based advertising by using your mobile device settings where available.

If you opt out of interest-based advertising as described above, please note that you will need to renew your opt-out choices if you use a different browser or erase cookies from your browser, reset your device’s advertising identifier, or use a different mobile device. Also, any settings available in your web browser to block cookies may not block the use of non-cookie technologies as described above. Please note that electing to opt out will not stop advertising from appearing in your browser or applications. It may make the ads you see less relevant to your interests.

Your Rights.

  • For California residents, the right to know the categories of personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell and share about you, the categories of sources from which we collected your personal information, our purposes for collecting or selling your personal information, the categories of your personal information that we have either sold, shared, or disclosed for a business purpose, and the categories of third parties with which we have disclosed personal information.

  • The right to opt out of our sale(s) of your personal information, including sharing for targeted (also called “cross-context behavioral”) advertising. Please note that if you opt out of certain practices, we may be unable to provide you with some Services.
  • The right to request that we correct inaccuracies in the personal information we have collected about you.

While California law provides California residents with an opt-out opportunity related to sensitive personal information, Slickdeals uses and discloses sensitive personal information, as defined under the California Privacy Rights Act, only for purposes permitted by that law that do not require an opt-out opportunity.

You also have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for the exercise of the above privacy rights.
