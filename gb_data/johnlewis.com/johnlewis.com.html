Father's Day

Celebrate Dad this Father's Day with our thoughtful range of Fathers Day gifts. We understand that sometimes life can be busy and time can slip away, so we even have a curated section of last minute Fathers Day gifts which are all next day delivery, ensuring youve got until the day before to find the perfect present for him. Spoil him with one of our luxurious Fathers Day hampers, filled with delicious treats or his favourite tipple, or opt for some Fathers Day chocolates for those dads who have a sweet tooth. If you know an expectant father or someone who has just become a dad, you can even peruse our range of thoughtful gifts for new dads. No present is complete without a heartfelt card browse our collection of Father's Day cards to express your love and appreciation. Make this Father's Day truly special with John Lewis.

Summer of Sport

Enjoy the summer of sport with a little help from John Lewis. As England and Scotland take part in Euro 2024, why not update your home entertainment by browsing our range of TVs, speakers and soundbars? You can even take part in your own football games by taking advantage of our range of sports equipment. Transform your garden into the perfect fan zone with comfortable garden furniture sets, and keep the excitement going with BBQs and pizza ovens for delicious outdoor feasts. Add to the ambience with stylish outdoor lights and keep the party warm into the night with a fire pit. If you prefer to take the fun outside of your home, our picnic sets are perfect for a leisurely day at the park. Discover everything you need today to ensure youre well-prepared for a summer of sport and relaxation.

Wedding Season

Celebrate wedding season in style with John Lewis. Make a statement with the perfect ensemble from our collection of women's wedding fashion. We offer stunning wedding dresses as well as bridesmaid dresses, whilst for those attending your big day, our curated selection of wedding guest dresses features an array of timeless designs and contemporary cuts. Add a flourish from our selection of wedding hats and fascinators, and complete your ensemble with our stunning range of wedding guest shoes. From classic pumps to chic sandals, we have everything you need to dance the night away. You can even browse our wedding outfit rental options for a selection of stunning attire. For little ones, you can pick from adorable flower girl outfits and page boy ensembles that are sure to melt hearts and complement the wedding party. Dress the groom and his party with our men's wedding fashion collection. From impeccably tailored mens wedding suits to sleek accessories, ensure they look their best as they stand by the bride's side. If youre looking for the perfect present, our range of wedding gifts includes useful homeware alongside personalised keepsakes, to help you find the ideal token to celebrate the happy couple's union.
