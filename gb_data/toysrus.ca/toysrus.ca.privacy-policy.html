When you visit our websites, the "R" Us Family collects certain information by automated means, using technologies such as cookies, pixel tags, browser analysis tools, server logs and web beacons.

Information collected by Cookies and Other Technologies

Cookies are small text files containing a unique identification number that are downloaded to your computer/device when you visit our websites. Cookies identify your computer/device browser to our computers each time you visit one of our websites. We use cookies to collect technical information about your visit to our websites (such as the IP address for your device, which browser you used to view our websites, the site you came from, keywords searched, the number of page views, the time you spend on our pages, the advertisements and products you have seen, whether you have clicked on those advertisements, and if you choose to make a purchase after viewing or clicking an advertisement). Pixel tags and web beacons are tiny graphic images placed on our website pages or in our emails that allow "R" Us Family members to determine whether you have performed a specific action. For example, when you access these pages or open or click an email, the pixel tags and web beacons inform us of that action.

A Flash cookie is a small data file placed on a computer or device using Adobe® Flash® or a similar technology that may be built into your computer or device or downloaded or installed by you to your computer or device. They help "R" Us Family members improve our websites by measuring which areas are of greatest interest to customers. Flash cookies are different from browser cookies and are managed through a different interface than the one provided by your web browser.
