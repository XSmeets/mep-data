June 7, 2024

Labour Force Survey, May 2024

Employment was little changed in May (+27,000; +0.1%) and the employment rate fell 0.1 percentage points to 61.3%.

Continue reading...

June 6, 2024

Canadian international merchandise trade, April 2024

In April, Canada's merchandise exports increased 2.6%, while imports rose 1.1%. As a result, Canada's merchandise trade deficit with the world narrowed from $2.0 billion in March to $1.0 billion in April.

Continue reading...

June 5, 2024

Time use and teleworkers: Highlights from the 2022 Time Use Survey

New data from the 2022 Time Use Survey are now available, including a study on telework and time use. In the context of the rise in working from home, this study examines how teleworking may be linked to time use—such as time in sleeping, free time, and unpaid work and care—as well as well-being.

Continue reading...

June 3, 2024

Provincial and Territorial Cultural Indicators, 2022

There was continued recovery in Canada's culture and sport sectors in 2022 following the initial COVID-19 pandemic restrictions put in place in the early part of 2020. Nominal gross domestic product of culture and sport increased in all provinces and territories in 2022.
