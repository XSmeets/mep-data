Kohl's Cookies

When you visit any website, it may store or retrieve information on your browser, mostly in the form of cookies. This information might be about you, your preferences or your device and is mostly used to make the site work as you expect it to. The information does not usually directly identify you, but it can give you a more personalized web experience. Under law, California and Virginia residents have the right to tell us not to “sell” their personal information. At Kohl’s, the only “sales” we make (as defined by Applicable law) are to share certain information among our affiliates consisting of cookies stored on your browser to provide more relevant ads to you on our sites, apps, and on other sites that you visit. On this page you can choose not to allow some types of cookies. Click on the different category headings to find out more and change the default settings. However, blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience of the site and the services we are able to offer.

Required Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not work. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

At Kohl’s, the only "sharing" or “sales” we make (as defined by applicable law) is the sharing of certain customer online information among our affiliates consisting of cookies stored on your browser to provide more relevant ads to you on our sites, apps, and on other sites that you visit.
