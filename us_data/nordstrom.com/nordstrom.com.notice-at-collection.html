Nordstrom Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2024

Using your personal information to provide you with the service you expect from Nordstrom.

At Nordstrom our customers are at the heart of what we do; we want to provide products and services that help each of our customers feel good and look their best, and this extends to our customers having confidence in how we handle their personal information. We take the trust you place in us very seriously, no matter whether you share personal information with us when visiting or making a purchase at our stores, reaching out to us by phone or email, using our websites ("Websites"), or our mobile applications ("Apps"). Within this policy, we will refer to the Websites, Apps, in-store activities, and communications with us collectively as the "Services." The goal of this Privacy Policy ("Policy") is to explain how Nordstrom, Inc., including our affiliates, subsidiaries, and family of brands (Nordstrom and Nordstrom Rack) (collectively, "Nordstrom", "we" or "us"), collect and use your personal information, who we disclose it to and when, and the tools and options available to you to manage that information.

This Policy applies to Nordstrom Services, and any sites, mobile apps or products that display or link to this Policy. It does not apply to any website, mobile app, service, or product that does not display or link to this Policy or that contains its own privacy policy. If you are the holder of a Nordstrom branded credit or debit card, please also review the Nordstrom Debit Card Privacy Notice and Nordstrom Credit Card Privacy Notice for more information about how Nordstrom Card Services and our banking partner use personal information associated with your Nordstrom branded credit or debit card.

How we collect personal information

We collect personal information in various ways, both directly and indirectly. For example, when you send us postal mail, email our customer care team, fill out a form on our Websites, place an order through the Websites or Apps, or provide information to one of our in-store team members, we collect your information directly from you. We may also collect your personal information indirectly through automated means, for example, when you interact with our Websites or use our Apps, we may use technologies to collect information about how you use the Websites or Apps. We may also receive information about you from third parties including from our affiliates and subsidiaries, social media platforms or from our advertising partners.

We collect personal information when you provide it

We may collect personal information directly from you or your device as follows:

  • When you register for a Nordstrom account, it is necessary for you to provide personal information that includes your name and email address. You may choose to provide us with additional personal information, including billing, mailing, and shipping addresses, phone number, and a payment method (e.g., credit card). If you make purchases through the Websites or Apps, you will also create a username and password, information that on subsequent visits helps us confirm your identity and makes it easier for you to make purchases on our Websites and Apps.
  • If you join our Nordy Club, in addition to the Nordstrom account information you provide (as noted above), we will also need your mobile phone number.
  • If you apply for a credit account or to manage an existing credit or debit account, you will need to provide your name, mailing address, email address, and phone number. You will also need to provide other information needed to assess your credit worthiness and verify your identity, such as date of birth, social security number, income, and employer information. This information is necessary for us to process your application, manage your account, and provide you with credit-related services. Please note that if you choose to link your card to another third-party website, they may use your information in different ways and have privacy policies that differ from ours; please refer to their privacy policies to understand how they collect and handle your information and your choices. For additional information about use of your personal information associated with a Nordstrom credit account, please see Nordstrom Credit Card Privacy Notice and Nordstrom Debit Card Privacy Notice.
  • When you shop with us, either through the Websites or App, we store your browsing history, what you searched for, including when you use our style personalization tools, what you have saved for later, and when you ask us to notify you about items that go on sale or come back in stock and what you bought (if you make a purchase).
  • When you interact with Nordstrom, including with a member of our sales team, visit a store, contact our Customer Care team, participate in a contest or sweepstakes, respond to a survey, engage in a promotional activity, register to attend an event or appointment, or apply for employment (applicants have access to our Careers Privacy Policy, which contains information on our collection and use of applicant and employee data).
  • When you sign up to receive emails, including marketing communications.
  • When you visit a store, we may collect video recordings and photographs of you that we use for security, to detect and prevent fraud, prevent product loss or damage, report incidents, and for operational purposes.

We collect personal information from third-party sources

To better understand your preferences, operate our business, and provide you with products and services we believe you will love, we may receive your personal information from the following third parties:

  • Nordstrom, Inc. and the Nordstrom family of brands and companies you have interacted with.
  • Online social networks if you follow Nordstrom, Nordstrom Rack, or any of our brands on social media sites, for example, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok or Pinterest. We may collect personal information when you communicate with us within the social media sites or use functionalities, plugins, widgets, or tools from social media platforms or networks in connection with our Websites (e.g., to log into an account, or to share inspiration, finds, purchases, and other content with your friends and followers on social media) or when you click "Share This" or "Like" buttons. We think it is important to point out that if you make a post on a social media site, such as on Facebook, Instagram, or Pinterest, or identify us in your social media feed by tagging us using a hashtag (#) or "at" (@), your personal information may be publicly available and is subject to the privacy policies of those social media sites. We recommend you review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit to understand the data collection and practices of those third parties.
  • Third-party service providers that support our business, including companies that help with our marketing efforts, analytics providers, advertising networks and cooperatives, third parties that share information with us about the different devices you use online, reviews you have posted on third-party sites about products we offer, and other third parties that we collaborate or work with.

We collect personal information using automated technologies

Personal information is also collected through automated technologies and shared with us when you visit our Website, use our Apps, open an email, or click on one of our advertisements. 

We automatically create website and mobile application log files, which are created when you access our Website and/or Apps. In addition, automated data collection technologies—such as cookies, beacons, tags, software development kits (SDK), and scripts—are used by us to analyze trends, administer the Websites and Apps, and track users' movements around the Websites and Apps. We, and our third-party service providers, also use these technologies to gather demographic information about our user base as individuals and in the aggregate.

A "cookie" is a small text file that is stored on an individual's computer and downloaded to the computer or mobile device when they visit a website. Cookies then send information back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognizes that same cookie. We use the following types of cookies: 

  • Essential Cookies are required to make our Websites and Apps function properly and cannot be turned off in our systems. If you block these cookies, portions of our Websites or Apps may not function properly.
  • Performance and Functional Cookies allow us to count visits and sources of traffic on our Websites and Apps so we can measure and improve performance and functionality. They help us know which pages of our Websites are most and least popular, which features of the Apps are used most, and how visitors move around the Websites and Apps.
  • Targeted Advertising Cookies may be set through our Websites by our advertising partners. They may be used by advertising companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other websites. Please see Targeted Advertising in the Your options and legal disclosures section for information about how you can opt out of these cookies.
  • Social Media Cookies allow you to share information using social media sharing buttons on social networks like Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Instagram, or Twitter. This information may then be used by the social media network to deliver targeted advertising to you.

We may combine information collected automatically (as described above) from your interactions with our Services and your activity on third-party sites with other data (including personal information). 

If you wish to opt out of allowing certain cookies, you may do so by changing your browser settings so that cookies from our Websites and Apps cannot be set (see Targeted Advertising in the Your options and legal disclosures section below for your options). Please note that if you delete, block, or otherwise restrict cookies, or use a different computer or Internet browser, you will need to renew your cookie management choices.

How we use personal information

We use personal information, including information that we combine from your interactions with our Websites and Apps, in store, your device, or third parties, to conduct our business and to provide you with the best possible products, services, and experiences. We know your personal information is important to you, so we want to be transparent about the purposes for which we use that information. Here the ways we use your personal information: 

  • To provide you access to the Websites and Apps, including to service your account(s) and memberships, and maintain the security of both.
  • To provide you with products and services, including to fulfill your orders, process your payments, and provide you with products and services that we think you will like.
  • To respond to or notify you, including when you place an order, initiate text messaging as a Nordstrom credit card holder, obtain information about your credit account (including balance, available credit and other similar information), obtain a QR code in store for same-day purchases, reach out to us with a comment, request or question, enter a contest or sweepstakes, complete a survey, consent to a store team member emailing, texting or calling you about products and services you have asked about or purchased, and to let you know when an order or alteration is ready, to notify you of updates to the App or, when you elect, to let you know an item is back in stock.
  • To personalize your experiences with us, including to remember your interests and preferences, customize the products and services we share with you, facilitate interactions with our stylists and store team members, track and categorize your activity and interests on the Websites and Apps, including identifying and linking the different devices you use, and to enrich your experience in our stores, on the Websites and the Apps.
  • For marketing and advertising, including to send you marketing and advertising communications tailored to your interests and more general in nature.
  • To offer you personalized content when you are near our store or store systems, have not disabled location sharing within your device settings, have installed any of our Apps and your device accesses our store Wi-Fi services.
  • For analytics, including to understand how you use our Websites and Apps, how you shop with us, the methods, and devices you use to access our Websites and Apps, and to make improvements to our Websites and Apps.
  • To manage our Nordy Club loyalty program, including to enroll new members and manage existing loyalty accounts.
  • For business purposes, including to operate and improve our business and business activities, for research, to maintain our programs, accounts, and records; to understand your satisfaction with our products and services including the Websites and Apps; to detect and prevent fraud or misuse of our services; and for any other business purpose permitted by law.
  • For legal and safety purposes, including to defend or protect us, you, our customers, or third parties, from harm or in legal proceedings; maintain the security of our customers, employees, and property; respond to court orders, lawsuits, subpoenas, and government requests; address legal and regulatory compliance; and notify you of product recalls or safety issues.

Your options and legal disclosures

We recognize that you want choice not only in the products and services we offer but also in how your personal information is used and shared. We currently offer the following choices and options:

  • Viewing your personal information. You can access your name, email address, billing address, shipping addresses, payment methods, transaction information (orders, purchases, and returns), Nordy Club information and email by logging into your account on the Websites and Apps.
  • Updating your personal information. You can also correct or update your name, email, billing or shipping addresses, some shopping preferences (such as preferred store location) and payment methods by logging into your account on the Websites. You may also make a request to update your personal information by writing to us at the email or postal mailing address specified in the Contact us section below.
  • Targeted advertising. You may opt out of the use of information about your browsing activities on our Services to show you ads on other websites and services by adjusting your settings for each device and browser you use with the Websites and within each App you use. To opt out of targeted advertising, you must submit a request using our online form and adjust settings for each device and browser you use with the Websites and within each App you use. To adjust settings for Nordstrom and Nordstrom Rack, please go to our opt-out page. Alternatively, you may visit our Services using a legally recognized device or browser-based universal opt-out mechanism such as the Global Privacy Control, which will opt out the sharing of your personal information at a browser level. You may also opt out of targeted advertising by certain companies that participate in third-party industry groups by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative consumer opt-out page and Digital Advertising Alliance of Canada opt-out tools. In addition, your device settings may offer you additional privacy settings when using our Apps; please see the device settings for details. Please note that even if you opt out of receiving interest-based advertising, you will still receive advertisements, but they will be more general in nature.
  • Email choices. You can opt out of marketing or advertising emails by following the unsubscribe process included in the promotional email you received or by logging into your account and indicating your preference. You may also request to opt out of marketing or advertising emails by contacting us at privacy@nordstrom.com. Please keep in mind that you if you opt out of receiving marketing messages, you will still receive transactional and service-related emails from us (such as emails related to your order, your membership in our Nordy Club, correction of personal information, password reset requests, reminder emails you have requested, updates to our terms and policies and other similar communications essential to your transactions on the Websites or Apps) that may be necessary for us to make the Websites and Apps available to you or respond to your inquiries. For questions about service or transactional emails related to your Nordstrom credit card, please call 1.800.964.1800, and for questions about service or transactional emails related to your Nordy Club membership, please call 1.800.446.4500.
  • Direct mail choices. You may opt out of receiving marketing or advertising by direct mail by logging into your account on our Websites and indicating your preference. Alternatively, you can contact us through one of the methods specified in the Contact us section below and provide the name and mailing address that you would like to opt out. Please note that these requests may take up to 8–10 weeks to become effective.
  • Telephone choices (calls and texts). You may opt out of receiving text messages by texting "Stop" at the end of any message you receive, or to opt out of marketing or advertising calls, by contacting us through one of the methods specified in the Contact us section below and sharing the phone number that you would like to opt out.
  • Mobile location choices. If you use our Apps, you may decline to provide location information through your mobile device settings or any prompt requesting location information. As noted above, we may collect location data (where your device settings allow) when you have installed the Apps and your device is near one of our stores, even if you are not currently using the Apps. You may prevent this by adjusting your mobile device settings to prevent sharing of location information on your device.

Identity verification requirement. The law requires that we validate the identity and legal authority associated with certain personal information requests, including for customers residing in states granting additional rights as set out below. As a result, we may require that you take additional steps to validate your identity and legal authority, particularly where you make a request through our online form or in writing (email or postal mail). If you make a request online or through our Customer Care center, we will send a message to the email address you provided in your request and you must validate your request within seven (7) days as instructed in that email. Your request will be considered validated once you have completed the email validation process or submitted additional information as requested. Should you fail to confirm your request within the relevant timeframe and/or fail to provide the additional information requested, your request will be considered unvalidated and closed.

We will provide a response to any personal information request within the timeframe required by law. If we cannot substantively respond to your request in a timely manner, we will notify you and provide the reason for the delay.

Under certain circumstances, we may not be able to fulfill your request, such as when doing so would interfere with our regulatory or legal obligations, where we cannot verify your identity, or if your request involves disproportionate cost or effort. But we will respond to your request within a reasonable time, as required by law, and provide an explanation.
