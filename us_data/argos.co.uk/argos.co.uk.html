Whether you prefer wooden or folding designs, our range includes reclining options primed for the patio or the beach. When the weather is right for it, bring everyone around for a barbecue – we have charcoal and gas models for easy outdoor cooking.
Keep the kids entertained outside with one of our paddling pools, with sizes to suit all sorts of outdoor spaces. Our trampolines add bouncy fun to the garden and are a popular way to keep the kids active.

Home refresh

Make cleaning a breeze with our vacuum cleaners and carpet cleaners. Ideal for tackling stubborn stains, help your carpets feel fresh and new. Or for hard floors, our steam cleaners offer a cleaning solution that leave surfaces sparkling.
If you’re having a declutter, we have lots of home storage solutions to help keep your space neat and tidy. Looking to kit out your kitchen? We have a wide range of appliances including the ever-popular air fryers to make mealtimes easier.
Summer is also a popular time to do DIY jobs around the home. Make light work of home improvements with our range of power tools.

Summer days

Get ready for the warmer weather with our range of fans. Our tower fans are great for all sorts of spaces, or you can choose desktop or bladeless designs.
Planning a trip? If you're heading on an adventure this summer, our large suitcases and luggage bags are ideal for your holiday essentials. With hard shell options for extra protection and sizes to fit airline requirements, we've got your travel needs covered. If you’ll be camping, our camping checklist will help you ensure you have the right kit for your stay. Keep yourself entertained on long journeys with an iPad or use a Kindle to store your favourite books to read on the sun lounger.

Play time

We have lots of outdoor toys to help kids make the most of the outdoors. Slides and swings bring the fun of the playground to your back garden. Kids and adults alike can enjoy a kick about outdoors with one of our footballs, and we also have accessories such as football goals for those matches in the back garden.
Get gaming with our range of games consoles and accessories. Pick up the power and iconic design of the PS5, complete with the PS5 DualSense controller for an action-packed, dynamic experience. Enjoy next-generation gaming with the Xbox Series consoles—flagship gaming machines supported by a wide range of immersive games. For a more portable gaming experience, check out the Nintendo Switch. There’s multiplayer fun with games like Animal Crossing, Breath of the Wild, and Mario Kart—all at your fingertips.
Dive into your gaming adventures in comfort with our range of ergonomic gaming chairs. With adjustable features to fit your setup, these chairs are ideal for those long gaming sessions. Check out the latest upcoming games to see what’s coming to your favourite console.
